About JVC Emas Sdn. Bhd.

JVC Emas Sdn. Bhd. is a dynamic and established company specializing in trading services, business consultancy, and quarry mining operations. Based in Malaysia, we serve contractors, developers, industrial clients, and government-linked projects with professionalism and integrity.

Our company operates a quarry facility in Ulu Mahang, Kedah, providing high-quality sand and aggregates to meet the demands of the construction and industrial sectors. Beyond mining operations, we offer comprehensive consultancy services in business strategy, project management, and industrial advisory.

We are committed to sustainable practices, operational excellence, and building long-term partnerships with our clients.
